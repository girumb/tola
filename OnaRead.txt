1. Log in to your Ona account at https://ona.io/
2. Go browse Ona's API, go to: https://ona.io/api/v1/data
3. Click on the data set that you'd like to set up as "READ" in TOLA-DATA.
4. Copy the url from your browser's address bar, which should look like: https://ona.io/api/v1/data/1112?format=json
5. Now, login to Tola-Data
6. Click on Load-Data -> Data Source
7. Click on "Create a New Source" button
8. Fill out the form: Don't forget to paste the copied url intot he "Source URL" field.
9. Click Save
10. Follow through the rest of the process
